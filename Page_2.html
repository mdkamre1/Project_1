Tourism & Attractions

Nepal is world-famous for the Himalayas, including Mount Everest, the highest peak on Earth.

  • Mount Everest & the Himalayas: trekking, base camps, scenic flights.
  • Kathmandu Valley: Swayambhunath, Pashupatinath, Patan and Bhaktapur Durbar Squares.
  • Pokhara: lakeside relaxation and Annapurna region access.
  • Lumbini: birthplace of Gautama Buddha — important pilgrimage site.
  • Chitwan National Park: rhinos, tigers, birding, Tharu cultural experiences.
