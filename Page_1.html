About Nepal

Nepal is a landlocked country in South Asia noted for its dramatic Himalayan peaks, rich cultural heritage, and diverse ethnic communities.

  • Home to Mount Everest — the highest mountain on Earth.
  • Kathmandu Valley: temples, palaces, and UNESCO heritage sites.
  • Popular trekking regions: Everest, Annapurna, Langtang.
